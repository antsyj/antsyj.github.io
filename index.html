🩸 Blood Donation Eligibility Quiz 🩸

🌐
🩸Are you feeling healthy and well today?
💉Are you between 17 and 65 years old?
⚕️Do you weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kg)?
🌡️Have you had a cold, flu, or infection in the last 7 days?
🩹Have you donated blood in the last 56 days?
💊Are you currently taking any medication that your doctor said would disqualify you?
🤒Have you ever had hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, or other blood-borne infections?
🩺Have you had any major surgery or tattoo in the past 12 months?
🤰Are you currently pregnant or breastfeeding?
💔Do you have any heart problems or chronic diseases your doctor has warned about?
